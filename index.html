// Who We Are

Built
Different.

4BN Offroad & Automotive is Trafalgar's 4WD specialist — a workshop with a genuine passion for four-wheel drive, but one that services all makes and models. Sedans, hatchbacks, utes or tourers — every vehicle gets the same honest, quality workmanship.

Whether you're a weekend warrior heading into the high country, a farmer relying on your ute every day, or just need a reliable local mechanic — we're across it all. And when it comes to 4WDs, from a routine service to a full suspension build, we go deeper than most.

Located in Trafalgar and servicing all of Baw Baw and Gippsland. No job too big or too small.
